
# Allocator Decision Engine – Refined Positioning (Fund-of-Funds Focus)

## Target Customer

Independent RIAs and Fund-of-Funds platforms evaluating external hedge funds, private credit, private equity, and alternative managers.

## Core Thesis

Fund-of-funds firms are not lacking performance analytics tools.
They are lacking structured, repeatable, defensible decision workflows.

The Allocator Decision Engine is:

A normalization and decision structuring platform purpose-built for alternative investment analysts.

## What Makes This Fund-of-Funds Specific

Unlike generic analytics tools, this product understands:

- Heterogeneous manager reporting formats
- Inconsistent time series lengths
- Liquidity gating terms
- Fee structure variance
- Strategy classification ambiguity
- Mandate-driven filtering logic

It embeds allocator workflow into the system.

## Positioning Statement

For fund-of-funds analysts who receive inconsistent manager data and must produce defendable IC memos, 
the Allocator Decision Engine converts messy fund reports into normalized, validated, ranked, and traceable investment decisions.

Unlike Excel or generic analytics dashboards, 
it enforces repeatable normalization and links every memo claim back to raw performance data.
